Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2024

Overview

Tensor Space is a browser extension that transforms your new tab into a desktop-like operating system environment. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Privacy First: Tensor Space is designed with privacy in mind. All your data is stored locally in your browser by default. Cloud sync is completely optional.

Data Collection

We collect and store the following data locally in your browser:

  • User preferences (wallpaper selection, theme settings, display options)
  • Files and notes you create in the File Explorer
  • TaskFlow kanban board data (columns, tasks, labels)
  • Pomodoro timer settings and history
  • Soundboard preferences and volume settings
  • Desktop icon positions and window states

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync by signing in.

Cloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google:

  • Your files, notes, and settings sync to our secure servers hosted on Supabase
  • We store your email address for authentication purposes
  • Your display name and profile picture (from Google) may be shown in the app
  • You can delete your synced data at any time from Settings
  • You can sign out at any time to stop syncing
Your Control: Cloud sync is entirely optional. The extension works fully offline without signing in.

Permissions Used

Tensor Space requires the following browser permissions:

Permission Purpose
tabs Detects new tab opens to display the Tensor Space desktop environment
storage Saves your preferences, files, and settings locally in your browser
identity Enables optional Google Sign-In for cloud sync features
alarms Powers the Pomodoro timer countdown and break reminders
notifications Alerts you when timer sessions complete or breaks begin
offscreen Enables background audio playback for the Soundboard feature

Third-Party Services

Tensor Space uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase - Database and authentication services for cloud sync. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • YouTube - Embedded audio for the Soundboard feature (ambient sounds). Audio is played through a sandboxed iframe. Google Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your data is only used to provide the functionality of the extension. We do not:

  • Sell your data to advertisers or data brokers
  • Use your data for targeted advertising
  • Share your data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • Track your browsing activity outside of the extension

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Local data is stored using Chrome's secure storage APIs
  • Cloud data is transmitted over HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication is handled securely through Google OAuth
  • Supabase provides enterprise-grade security for stored data

Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored indefinitely until you clear browser data or uninstall the extension
  • Cloud data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted upon request or account deletion

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the extension
  • Export your data from Settings
  • Delete your local data by clearing browser storage
  • Delete your cloud data from Settings or by contacting us
  • Opt out of cloud sync at any time by signing out

Children's Privacy

Tensor Space is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
